# Brand Guidelines — TheAltText

## Brand Identity

- Product: **TheAltText**
- Brand Family: **GlowStarLabs**
- Positioning: AI-powered accessibility compliance for image alt text at scale

## Voice and Tone

- Clear, practical, and compliance-focused
- Supportive for non-technical users
- Confident but not alarmist about legal/compliance risk

## Visual Direction

- Dark, earthy, low-blue-light palette
- High contrast text and controls for WCAG AAA readability
- Minimal motion and sensory-safe UI patterns

## Core Messaging

1. Generate high-quality alt text quickly
2. Improve ADA/WCAG compliance confidence
3. Scale accessibility workflows for teams and agencies

## Do / Don’t

### Do
- Emphasize accessibility outcomes and workflow speed
- Use inclusive language
- Show practical ROI (time saved, reduced manual effort)

### Don’t
- Claim guaranteed legal immunity
- Use fear-based language
- Sacrifice accessibility for visual flair
