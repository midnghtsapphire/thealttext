# Go-To-Market Plan — TheAltText

## Market Validation (Sourced)

1. **Accessibility is a legal and operational requirement**
   - DOJ ADA web accessibility guidance confirms public accommodations should provide accessible web content.
   - Source: https://www.ada.gov/resources/web-guidance/

2. **Regulatory pressure for digital accessibility is increasing**
   - The U.S. DOJ issued a final rule on web/mobile accessibility requirements for state and local governments under ADA Title II.
   - Source: https://www.ada.gov/resources/2024-03-08-web-rule/

3. **E-commerce image quality and discoverability matter**
   - Google Search Central recommends descriptive alt text for image understanding and accessibility.
   - Source: https://developers.google.com/search/docs/appearance/google-images#alt-text

## Target Segments

- Small-to-mid businesses with ADA/WCAG risk exposure
- Agencies managing many client websites
- E-commerce teams with large product-image catalogs
- Public-sector and education teams with compliance obligations

## Positioning

**TheAltText helps teams generate scalable, WCAG-aligned image descriptions faster than manual workflows, while supporting compliance reporting and API integration.**

## Pricing and Packaging

- Free tier: 50 images/month (adoption + trial)
- Pro tier: unlimited usage + API + advanced reporting

## Distribution Channels

1. Organic SEO content around alt text compliance and ADA readiness
2. Product-led onboarding from free tier to Pro conversion
3. Agency partnerships and developer API integrations
4. Browser extension usage path for in-workflow adoption
